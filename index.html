About Me

I'm a passionate graphic designer graduating from the prestigious design program in Florence, Italy. My education has immersed me in the rich artistic heritage of Renaissance masters while embracing cutting-edge digital design techniques.

Growing up surrounded by timeless art and architecture has shaped my aesthetic sensibility, while my international perspective brings fresh insights to every project. I specialize in brand identity, digital marketing materials, and user-centered design solutions.

I'm seeking opportunities to bring my unique blend of classical design principles and modern innovation to dynamic creative teams in the US market.
